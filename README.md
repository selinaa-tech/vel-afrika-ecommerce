# Vel'Afrika - E-Commerce Mobile App

Vel'Afrika is a mobile e-commerce platform and online marketplace that connects African producers and artisans with international customers.

The primary objective of the app is to maximize product purchases from customers worldwide by creating a frictionless, trust-driven shopping experience that combines e-commerce best practices with social engagement and light gamification.

The platform is designed and optimized for scalability, performance, and future feature expansion.

---

## Core Business Goal

Increase international product purchases and customer lifetime value by:

- Reducing purchase friction
- Building trust with producers and products
- Encouraging sharing and repeat purchases
- Increasing average order value through bundles and recommendations
- Using social interaction and rewards to improve engagement and conversion

---

## Design Principles

The application should follow the following design principles:

- Clean, simple, and intuitive user experience
- Light-colored background with a modern aesthetic
- Minimal visual clutter
- Large product imagery
- Clear calls-to-action
- Mobile-first design
- Fast navigation with minimal user actions
- Accessibility-friendly interfaces
- Conversion-focused layouts
- Visible trust signals throughout the user journey

---

## Customer Journey

```text
Discover Product
    ↓
Follow Producer
    ↓
Save / Share Product
    ↓
Earn Rewards
    ↓
Build Trust
    ↓
Add to Cart
    ↓
One-Click Purchase
    ↓
Review Product
    ↓
Invite Friends
    ↓
Repeat Purchase
```

Alternative linear flow:

```text
Discover Product → Follow Producer → Save/Share Product → Earn Rewards → Build Trust → Add to Cart → One-Click Purchase → Review Product → Invite Friends → Repeat Purchase
```

The app aims to provide a mobile-first experience that combines:

- E-commerce
